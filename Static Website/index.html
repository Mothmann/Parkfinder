How can I reset my password ?

Enter your Email address and click on Reset Password to receive an email to set your new password.

If I cancel my reservation is there any refund ?

Select the reservation you want to cancel. 

From the Details tab, select Change or cancel reservation and get your refund in three days.

Why didn't I receive my details payment ?

If you receive an email saying you’ve made a payment, but you don't receive your reservation, there's a lot of possibilities:

It may have been accidentally sent to the wrong email address. You could contact the sender to confirm that the payment was sent to the correct email address.

You may have the email address on your account, but you didn't confirm

How to use the QR Code ?

Once the reservation made, you have to print the QR code and go to the parking. There, you have to show your QR code to the application and it will scan it automatically.
